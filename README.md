# Hi there, I'm M U Sunny! 👋

### Co-founder of [Boipedia](https://boipedia.org/) | Wikipedian | Photographer | Cybersecurity Researcher

I am a multi-disciplinary professional and open knowledge advocate based in Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh 🇧🇩. I love combining technology, photography, and community-driven projects to document local heritage and share knowledge with the world.

- 🚀 **Co-founder of Boipedia:** Documenting and sharing information about Bengali books and literary works.
- ✍️ **Wikipedian:** Active member of the Wikimedia community, contributing to open knowledge and heritage documentation.
- 📸 **Photographer & Visual Storyteller:** Capturing moments and documenting the rich heritage of Bangladesh.
- 🛡️ **Cybersecurity Researcher:** Passionate about ethical hacking and cybersecurity research.
- 💻 **Web Designer & Developer:** Building modern, user-friendly interfaces and web solutions.
